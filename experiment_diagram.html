Experimental Setup

Dataset

  • Anti-UAV - Jiang et al. 2023
  • 318 RGB-T video pairs; RGB only used
  • 580k+ manually annotated bounding boxes
  • Split: 160 train / 67 val / 91 test
  • Test set from separate videos (independent)
  • Avg. target size <40px; day & night scenes
  • UAVs: DJI & Parrot; diverse backgrounds

Fog Generation

  • Atmospheric scattering: I(x) = J(x)t(x) + A(1−t(x))
  • β = 0.01i + 0.05, i ∈ {0,…,9}
  • Radial depth prior (no ground truth depth)
  • A = 0.5 (fixed atmospheric light)
  • 10 levels per frame; main eval: i = {2, 5, 9}

Model Architecture

  • YOLOv5m
  • Chosen for fast inference across all fog levels
  • Consistent with Munir et al. 2024 benchmark
  • Trained from scratch per condition
  • Validation: early stopping on val split
  • Input: normalised RGB [0,1] · Single-class: UAV

Evaluation

  • Mean Average Precision (mAP@ 0.5:0.95)
  • Precision & Recall per fog level
  • Size strata: Tiny [2,10), Small [10,30), Medium [30,50), Large [50+)
  • ΔmAP = mAPclear − mAPfog
Anti-UAV dataset (318 RGB-T video pairs, 580k+ manual annotations; 160 train / 67 val / 91 test; avg. target size <40px; day/night, diverse backgrounds): Jiang et al. 2023 - RGB modality only used in this study  |  Atmospheric scattering model (fog generation formula): Narasimhan & Nayar 2002; Cantor 1978  |  Fog severity framework (parametric β control, radial depth prior): Agarwal et al. 2024  |  YOLOv5m detector (architecture; adverse weather benchmark): Munir et al. 2024  |  Tiny UAV size definition (bounding bod size <10px): Huang et al. 2023
