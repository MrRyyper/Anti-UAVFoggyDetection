Experimental Setup

Dataset

  • Anti-UAV300 — Jiang et al. 2023
  • 318 RGB-T video pairs; RGB only used
  • 280k+ annotated RGB bounding boxes
  • Split: 160 train / 67 val / 91 test
  • Test set from separate videos (independent)
  • RGB median target scale 86px; day & night scenes
  • Diverse backgrounds (sky, buildings, trees)

Fog Generation

  • Atmospheric scattering: I(x) = J(x)t(x) + A(1−t(x))
  • β = 0.01i + 0.05, i ∈ {0,…,9} — all 10 levels
  • Radial depth prior (no ground-truth depth)
  • A = 0.5 fixed; sensitivity at A ∈ {0.3, 0.7}
  • Representative levels: i = {2, 5, 9}

Model Architecture

  • YOLOv5m (medium, ~21.2M params)
  • Architecture per Munir et al. 2024 benchmark
  • Fine-tuned from COCO-pretrained weights
  • 3 training seeds per model (mean ± std)
  • Early stopping on validation mAP@0.5:0.95
  • Input 640×640, normalised [0,1] · Single-class: UAV

Evaluation

  • mAP@0.5:0.95, mAP@0.5, Precision, Recall, F1
  • Per fog level and per size category
  • Size strata (s = √(w×h)): Small <50, Medium [50,90), Large ≥90
  • 27 tiny instances (<10px) merged into Small
  • ΔmAP = mAPclear − mAPfog (per model)
Anti-UAV300 dataset (318 RGB-T video pairs, 280k+ RGB annotations; 160 train / 67 val / 91 test; RGB median scale 86px; day/night, diverse backgrounds): Jiang et al. 2023 — RGB modality only used in this study  |  Atmospheric scattering model (fog generation formula): Narasimhan & Nayar 2002; Cantor 1978  |  Fog severity protocol (parametric β control, radial depth prior): Agarwal et al. 2024  |  YOLOv5m detector (architecture; adverse-weather benchmark): Munir et al. 2024  |  Size taxonomy (tiny threshold <10px, adapted to RGB scale distribution): Huang et al. 2024; Xie et al. 2025
